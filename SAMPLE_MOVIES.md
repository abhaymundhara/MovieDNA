## Tips for Best Results

1. **Use exact titles**: "The Dark Knight" works better than just "Dark Knight"
2. **Try popular films first**: They have more complete crew data in TMDB
3. **Avoid very obscure films**: Limited data may result in fewer recommendations
4. **Recent films work great**: Modern films have comprehensive credits
5. **English titles preferred**: Use the English release title for best results
